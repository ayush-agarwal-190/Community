Festivals Celebrated by the Marwari Community

Diwali - The Festival of Lights

Diwali is the most celebrated festival in the Marwari community. It marks the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. Click to read more about Diwali.

Holi - The Festival of Colors

Holi is a vibrant festival where people smear colors on each other and celebrate with joy and laughter. Click to read more about Holi.

Teej - The Festival of Monsoon

Teej is a significant festival for Marwari women, celebrated during the monsoon season. Click to read more about Teej.

Ganesh Chaturthi - The Birth of Lord Ganesha

Ganesh Chaturthi marks the birth of Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles. Click to read more about Ganesh Chaturthi.

Makar Sankranti - The Harvest Festival

Makar Sankranti is celebrated to mark the transition of the Sun into the zodiac sign of Capricorn. Click to read more about Makar Sankranti.
